Welcome to the Past Percent App User Manual

This app allows you to record your past residences and visualize the percentage of time you lived in each country with the help of a bar chart.

Getting Started

To get started, follow these steps:

  1. Open Past Percent in your browser.
  2. Sign up and login.
  3. Once you are logged in, you are able to add residences in the residence menu.

Recording Your Past Residences

In the Residence menu, you can add your past residences one by one by clicking on the "New Residence" button. Fill in the required information for each residence:

  • Country: Choose the name of the country where you lived.
  • City: Enter the name of the city where you lived.
  • Start Date: Enter the date you moved to that country (format: dd-mm-yyyy).
  • End Date: Enter the date you left that country (format: dd-mm-yyyy).
  • Reason: Specify the reason for living in that country (e.g., Motherland, Travel, Work, Study, etc.).

After entering the details, click on the "Submit" button to record the residence. You can repeat this process to add multiple past residences.

Viewing the Past Residences

To view your recorded past residences, navigate to the "Residences" menu and click on the Residences submenu. Here, you will see a list of all the residences you have added, displaying the country name, start date, end date, and the reason for living in that country. You can edit a residence by clicking on the Edit button. A redidence can be deleted by clicking on the Delete button.

Generating the Bar Chart

To see the percentage of time you lived in each country represented as a bar chart, click on the Diagram submenu on the "Residence" menu. You can see the world map where shows all countries that you ever resident in Red, others stay grey.
